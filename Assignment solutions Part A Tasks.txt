T2: Create a table Students with the following columns:
student_id (number, primary key)
student_name (varchar)
email (varchar, unique)
course (varchar)
fees (number)
join_date (date)

Solution:
SQL> create table student(student_id number(10) primary key,student_name varchar(20),email varchar(30) unique,course varchar(10),fees number(8),join_date date);
